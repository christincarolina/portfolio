Internship at Avenda Health

Avenda Health is a medical device startup whose mission is to create an intelligent platform for focal prostate cancer treatment while preserving quality of life. As a user experience design intern, I was responsible for the treatment planning system for the focal laser ablation hardware. I worked alongside a ux designer, full-stack developers and product managers, urologists for user research and usability testing. It was my first time conducting remote usability testing with physicians from Stanford University and John Hopkins School Of Medicine. ​ Note: Unfortunately, I am unable to publicly share the final product due to NDA reasons. However, if you would like to know more about my project, please contact me. I am able to provide the design process that I went through for this project.
