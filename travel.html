Planning a travel itinerary is time-consuming and can be overwhelming.


Problem

Users need to consolidate travel information from a variety of sources. The current solution requires users to google activities pertaining to their individual interests.

Solution

What if we can provide a platform for users to connect with like-minded individuals who have similar interests and have gone to the places we want to go to? Wouldn't it eradicate the time to plan?

Challenge

What is the incentive of posting on your travel itinerary in this platform?


Travelers (in general) are more inclined to actively seek and listen to advices from close friends/family who have done them.

5/8 interviewees mentioned they prefer to seek travel recommendations from someone they know who’ve been to the destination they are planning to go before looking at information online.
