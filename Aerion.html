Aerion Supersonic

The Aerion AS2 is a supersonic business jet under development by Aerion Corporation. It is a 8-12 seater.

Part of the class final project is to evolve Aerion;s brand identity language. .

Strategy

Develop an empowering, energetic, and permium, identity and a promotional campaign of their AS2 jet launch for 2020.

Current logo

Brand Story

The new identity reflects Aerion's mission to be the first and only company to introduce a modern, quiet, efficient supersonic engine.The new logo creates new global possibilities achieved through supersonic mobility. The new typeface evokes a modern and clean look that provides an authentic and luxurious experience to our customers. With Aerion, customers can reach their true potential and become a better version of themselves.

Inspiration

The inspiration behind this logo system comes from shock waves, connection to people and the capability of reaching to new heights. When an object travels faster than the speed of sound in a medium, a cone shaped region of high pressure called a shock wave trails behind it. No sound is heard because the object is traveling faster than the sound it produces. The intertwining circles latched together are inspired by the capability of connecting people who are miles apart. Each ring captures the potentiality of overcoming time and communication barriers..
