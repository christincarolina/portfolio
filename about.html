About Me

Always keeping up with my endless tabs of "UX Trends" and my love for Peet's Coffee.

Selamat datang! (Welcome! in Bahasa Indonesia)

I am a recent graduate from ArtCenter College of Design in Pasadena, CA. I am a user experience designer whose work is driven by research insights and user’s feedback. I feel most satisfied when I know I am improving my user’s lives through my designs. I've always been interested in understanding the business goals of my projects.  I am collaborative, passionate and a critical thinker in my approach to design and product strategy.  I am constantly iterating and learning through my user's feedback. I believe that storytelling is necessary in design as it helps us relate and be empathetic towards one another. ​ In the past, I have designed for Avenda Health, Rootstrap Inc, as well as freelanced for early-stage startups (The Shonet). When I'm not designing, I'm either playing badminton, reading, or creating my take on dessert recipes. Yum. ​
